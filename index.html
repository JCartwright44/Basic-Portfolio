About Me

Jason hails from Chicago, where he grew up and lived most of his life before moving to Sacramento with his family. Jason has always had a passion for coding and front-end web development. He lives with his wife and two small children in Gold River a suburb of Sacramento.

Working as a User Experience Researcher at SMUD, the Sacrament Municipal Utility District, Jason has worked hand in hand with User Experience designers and front end web developers, creating the experiences on the award winning SMUD website.

Jason began his budding web development career by attending the UC Davis Extension Coding Bootcamp. With this bootcamp, Jason has honed the skills necessary to excel at any development task anywhere within the stack. He is excited to keep on learning and making an impact on your company's stack.

Jason has created online apps and games that are currently on the app store for Apple and Google. These games feature the breath and width of Jason's knowledge, and are exceptional sellers.

Having parlayed the Bootcamp experience into a lucritive second job, Jason is eager to continue learing new languages and moving forward in his career, working remotely and exceeding clients' expectations.
